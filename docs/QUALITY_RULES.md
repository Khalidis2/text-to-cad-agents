# Quality Rules

## Bambu A1 0.4 mm nozzle baseline

- Minimum wall: 0.8 mm
- Minimum structural thickness: 3.0 mm
- Minimum raised text depth: 0.6 mm
- Minimum readable text height: 6 mm
- Recommended mirror-hanger hole: 5 mm
- Minimum hole diameter: 4.5 mm
- Minimum material above hole: 3 mm
- Default orientation: flat on back
- Required output: STL, STEP, SVG preview, report JSON

## Typography limitation

CadQuery text depends on local font availability. Arabic and mixed-script products should eventually use shaped vector outlines for final commercial typography.
